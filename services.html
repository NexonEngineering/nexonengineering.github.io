Our Services

What we offer

Electrical & Machinery

Machine Repair, Assembly & Maintenance

We provide complete solutions for industrial machinery, including repair, assembly, and preventive maintenance. Our team handles mechanical and electrical troubleshooting, emergency breakdowns, and long-term factory maintenance contracts to ensure smooth and uninterrupted production.

Industrial Electrical Wiring

We undertake all types of industrial electrical installations, including control panel wiring, system upgrades, VFD setup and programming, and motor control systems. Our services also cover fault finding, rewiring, and concealed or floor wiring solutions for factories and commercial buildings.

Automation & Electronics

Industrial Automation

We design and implement custom automation systems to increase production efficiency. Our services include PLC and HMI programming, automated system design, retrofitting old machines with modern automation, and integrating sensors and safety systems for improved performance.

Industrial Electronics Repairs

We repair industrial electronic boards, automation modules, sensors, and other control components to restore full functionality and ensure reliable machine performance.

Mechanical & Fabrication

Mechanical Fabrication

We handle all types of mechanical fabrication work, including welding, machine part repairs, component modification, and custom fabrication of brackets, frames, and conveyor structures to suit specific industrial needs.

Spare Parts & Components

We supply a range of industrial electrical components, machine parts, and accessories to support maintenance, repairs, and production requirements.

Industrial Support & Services

On-Site Technical Support

We offer reliable on-site technical assistance, including on-call machine servicing, troubleshooting visits, consultancy for new installations, and 24/7 emergency breakdown support to minimize downtime and ensure continuous operations.

Industrial Services

Our industrial services cover factory setup and commissioning, preventive maintenance planning, power distribution installation, and energy-efficient solutions. We help optimize workflow, reduce energy costs, and support overall factory operations.

Compressed Air Line Fixing & Maintenance

Professional compressed air line installation, repair, and maintenance services for industrial environments. We ensure leak-free pipelines, optimal air flow, improved energy efficiency, and reliable pneumatic system performance.

Solar & Security Systems

Solar Engineering

We provide professional solar installation services for factories and businesses. This includes solar panel wiring, testing, commissioning, and hybrid or off-grid system setup. (Labour-only work available for clients who supply equipment.)

CCTV Installation & Service

We provide CCTV installation, repair, maintenance, and system configuration services. Whether upgrading existing systems or installing new setups, we ensure secure and high-quality surveillance solutions for homes and businesses.
