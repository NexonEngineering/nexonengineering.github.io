About Us

Who We Are

Nexon Engineering is a trusted provider of industrial repair, maintenance, and automation services. We specialize in machine servicing, industrial electrical work, and custom engineering solutions designed to improve productivity and reliability. With a commitment to precision, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we deliver high-quality engineering services that keep your operations running at their best. At Nexon Engineering, we believe in quality workmanship, transparent service, and long-term solutions. Whether it is a routine service, a complex breakdown, a new installation, or a complete factory maintenance contract, we are committed to delivering results that exceed expectations.

Driven by innovation and guided by professionalism, we aim to be a trusted partner for industries across Sri Lanka, providing services that enhance operational excellence and support sustainable growth. Nexon Engineering – Engineering Your Success with Precision, Reliability, and Integrity.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and high-quality engineering services in machine repair, maintenance, automation, and industrial electrical work.

To continuously innovate and adopt advanced technologies to deliver long-lasting solutions for our clients.

To ensure customer satisfaction through professionalism, integrity, and transparent service.

To develop a skilled workforce committed to safety, excellence, and continuous improvement.

To build long-term partnerships with industries by offering consistent support and value-driven engineering solutions.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka’s leading and most trusted engineering services provider, delivering innovative, sustainable, and high-precision industrial solutions that empower businesses to achieve operational excellence.
